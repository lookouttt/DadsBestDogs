Where We'll Be

As a new business, we're starting with a simplified menu that is sure to grow over time. Come see us for a delicious all-beef hot dog or polish sausage. Add some chili, sauerkraut, or a variety of other options. Match it with a variety of different chips. And finally, wash it down with a nice bottled water, soda, energy drink, or Coca-Cola.

Our Story

Dad's Best Dogs is the newest food truck in Kitsap County started by Dwayne Partridge and Darren Gettel. Dwayne and Darren are a couple of fun-loving guys who love to serve. This love to serve and create relationships is clearly seen when you see them at the Dad's Best Dogs food truck.

Dwayne, a former Californian, and Darren, a former Canadian, met years ago at a church in Kitsap County. They quickly became the best of friends. They're a couple of dads who love God, people, and a good backyard barbecue.

Some of their favorite foods at a barbecue have always been hot dogs and sausages. Now they're taking that love of all things 'dog' and bringing it all around Kitsap County. So come and visit Dwayne and Darren as they bring their delicious dogs to a location near you. You're sure to have a laugh or two watching this dynamic duo put together some of the best dogs around.
