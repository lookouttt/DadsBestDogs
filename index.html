Our Story

Dad's Best Dogs is the brain child of Dwayne Partridge and Darren Gettel. Dwayne is an entrepreneur at heart. A few years ago he started working on a food truck and found that he really enjoyed it. He loves meeting all the people that come up to get food from the truck.

Darren has a history of working at various trade shows in the area. Through this, he has seen the need to have available food options at these shows. He also has gotten to know a lot of the people at the various venues.

Dwayne and Darren have known each other for several years. As they developed their friendship, they started thinking about the idea of getting into business together. Through these conversations they decided that a food truck would be the perfect choice. And what better item to start with than one that they both loved, hot dogs and sausages.

So after much preperation, Darren and Dwayne got all of the necessary steps taken care of to start this new venture. So get prepared to meet them as they take their love of hot dogs all around Kitsap County.
